VR in the Media

VR Usage

Virtual reality is utilized in many new media forms. As our society continues to advance, virtual reality

will replace most current usages of media.

VR as a Viewing Platform

Virtual reality is a unique way to view video games, television shows, and movies. This will further allow

important life moments to be captured as if you are there in real time.

VR Music

This technology has formed part of experimental sound displays and sound installations. Another use is virtual

reality musical instruments where the person can interact with these instruments as a new type of performance.

VR in film and TV

Virtual reality has been featured in several film and television programmes. It is often used to illustrate the

concept of being trapped within the machine (i.e, cyberspace), or as a form of advanced technology.

VR Art

There are artists who use virtual reality to explore certain ideas or concepts. They create a three dimensional

environment as a form of communication with the audience. One example is the work of Kenneth Rinaldo

who uses robotics and augmented reality to explore ideas related to the human-technology boundary.
