Case Studies

A South Korean documentary reunited a grieving mother with her dead daughter—in VR.

Jang Ji-sung, who lost her daughter to blood cancer in 2016, welcomed the opportunity to see her daughter again—even if it was just in VR.

Jang may not have been the first mother to lose a child, but she is perhaps the first who got to meet her deceased daughter as a VR simulacrum,

one that took almost a year to create.

Virual Reality: Immersion aids recall.

Study participants trained via VR demonstrated a 9% better information recall and made 41% fewer errors than those trained via Non-VR.

Published by: University of Maryland

National News Media uses Virtual Reality Technology for Better Viewer Engagement.

RTV Slovenia used VR technology to improve engagement of their online content with the goal of proving that immersive news viewing holds more

attention than traditional desktop viewing. Upon completion, RTV recorded more than 5000 views over 48 hours with an average of 2 minutes of engagement

time per session.
